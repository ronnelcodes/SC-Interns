SC Interns is designed to take your passion for God and desire to serve and apply to hands on ministry here at Spark Church Kelowna. Whether you enjoy filming, graphic design, singing, budgeting, writing or meeting new people, then you’ve come to the right place. Perhaps you don’t know what you like or what your good at. But you are willing to find out. Then the internship is perfect for you! You will be engaged in so many learning opportunities that will stretch you as a leader, creative, and Christ follower. This will be a year you will never forget.

(This is an unpaid internship at Spark Church Kelowna. There is no compensation for the time of Internship and no promise of employment at the end of the Internship.)
